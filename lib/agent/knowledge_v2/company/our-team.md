Carolyn, BCBA, LBA Clinical Director Carolyn is a NYS licensed and board-certified behavior analyst. She received her BA and MA in Psychology from Adelphi University. She completed her BCBA coursework through Florida Institute of Technology. Carolyn has been working with individuals on the autism spectrum and other developmental disabilities for over twelve years. Carolyn has worked in school settings, group homes and family’s homes over the last twelve years. She has extensive experience working with all age groups and is dedicated to helping others meet their highest potential. She has been involved in research and has presented at numerous conferences over the last few years. Julia, Clinical Intake Coordinator Julia holds a Masters in School Counseling from LIU Post and a Bachelor’s of Science in Child and Family Studies. She has been working with children and parents for over 15 years, and has experience advocating for children in school districts while being a part of Committee of Special Education and Individualized Education Program Meetings in Elementary through High School aged children. She has experience teaching since 2016 and has been working with children on the autism spectrum and other developmental disabilities while obtaining her masters. Julia has a strong passion for helping individuals and families to the best of her ability. As an intake coordinator she makes sure all parents and care givers feel knowledgeable about ABA therapy and offers support and guidance throughout the process. Adam Marketing Director Adam Lindenblatt, our Marketing Director, brings a unique blend of skills and experience to Achievement Behavior Services. With a Bachelor's degree from Binghamton University and diverse past experiences in real estate, venture capital, and music management, Adam possesses a multifaceted perspective that fuels our marketing strategies. Having worked as a recruiter for Achievement for over three years, Adam deeply understands our organization and is committed to our mission. His dedication to excellence and ability to connect with our target audience has driven our marketing initiatives forward. Ariella, Program Director Ariella oversees recruiting, interviewing, and evaluating potential new staff. She also provides case management of clients participating in the program. Ariella has her Bachelor’s degree in Sociology from Queens College and has worked in both the fashion and health fields working in administration and customer care. When she is not at work, Ariella can be found spending quality time with her husband and children.
