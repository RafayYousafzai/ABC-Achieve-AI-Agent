In this guide, we’ll walk you through exactly how it works, what to expect, and how ABS can help you every step of the way. What is ABA Therapy? Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) is a proven, evidence-based therapy that helps children with autism develop critical skills like communication, social interaction, and independence. ABA therapy is widely recognized as one of the most effective treatments for autism spectrum disorder (ASD). Does New York Medicaid Cover ABA Therapy? Yes. As of 2022, New York State Medicaid and most Medicaid Managed Care Plans are required to cover ABA therapy for eligible children. Whether your child has straight Medicaid or is enrolled through a Managed Care plan, there are options available to access ABA services.
