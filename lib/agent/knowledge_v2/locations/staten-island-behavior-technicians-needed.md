Signal: The Call Is Loud and Clear It’s easy to overlook Staten Island when talking about healthcare or therapy needs in NYC. But when it comes to autism care, Staten Island’s need is loud, clear, and growing. Families across the borough are seeking consistent, compassionate care especially for after-school hours when children need the most support. 2. Story: Families Are Waiting for Help Imagine being a parent of a child with autism, knowing ABA therapy could make a difference, but struggling to find someone nearby. We’ve seen firsthand the impact that a well-trained Behavior Technician can have not just on the child, but on the whole family. Each case is a story of progress, of learning, and of hope. These aren’t just numbers, they’re real people who need someone to show up and care. The goal of this hiring initiative is simple: to reduce wait times and deliver consistent care. If your family is in need of compassionate, personalized ABA therapy, Achievement Behavior Services (ABS) is actively expanding our capacity to serve children across Staten Island, Queens, and the entire New York region. 3. Surprise: Staten Island Is Ready You may not expect it, but Staten Island has one of the highest concentrations of open ABA cases in NYC. We have families ready. We have BCBAs in place. What we need are committed Behavior Technicians people with heart, reliability, and a desire to make a difference. And the surprise? These roles are flexible, well-paid, and incredibly rewarding. Staten Island is ready for growth. We are dedicated to filling this need for quality care by providing strong teams. Parents interested in starting services can check our options for ASD Evaluation in NY. Professionals ready to join the mission can view all open positions on our Careers Page. Solution: Your Role in the Bigger Picture This isn’t just a job. It’s a mission. By joining Achievement Behavior Services (ABS), you become part of a team that is pushing back against the shortage of support services for kids with autism. You’ll get the training, the support, and the mentorship you need to thrive. We’ve been proudly serving families since 2015 across New York, and we’re just getting started. 5. Shift: Change a Life, Starting Now The opportunity to make an impact is here and it starts in Staten Island. Whether you’re new to ABA or experienced, this is your chance to be part of something bigger. We need people who care. We need you.
