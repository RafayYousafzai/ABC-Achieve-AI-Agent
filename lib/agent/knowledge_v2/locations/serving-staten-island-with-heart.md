What Is ABA and How Does It Work? Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) is the gold standard in autism treatment. It helps children develop life-changing skills – from communication to social interaction, independence, and more. Here’s how it works: Assessment & Planning: A Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) creates a personalized treatment plan Hands-On Support: A trained Behavior Technician (BT) works directly with your child to implement the plan Ongoing Progress: The team adjusts and fine-tunes the program using real-time data and family input ABA is evidence-based, outcome-driven, and most importantly, centered on your child’s unique needs.
