What are ABA School Consultation Services? ABA School Consultation involves working with schools to support students using evidence-based ABA strategies that improve behavior, learning, and social success in the classroom. Who provides school consultation services? Services are led by Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) with experience supporting students in educational settings. Does ABA School Consultation replace in-school services? No. Consultation services are designed to complement existing school supports and help teams implement effective strategies consistently. Can ABA School Consultation support IEP goals? Yes. ABA strategies can be aligned with a student’s Individualized Education Program (IEP) goals when appropriate. How do we get started with ABA School Consultation? The first step is completing our form. Our team will review your information and connect with you to discuss next steps.
