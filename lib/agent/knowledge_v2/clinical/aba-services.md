Start Your Journey Personalized In-Home & Center Based ABA Services: Early Intervention Early intervention services are designed for children under the age of 3, a time when the brain is developing rapidly. Through tailored ABA therapy, we focus on strengthening early communication, improving social interactions, and gently shaping positive behaviors. Starting support early can make a lasting difference in your child’s developmental journey and set the foundation for future learning and growth. Our team works closely with families to create a plan that fits your child’s unique needs and milestones. Read More Social Skills Groups Social Skills Groups offer children a safe and supportive space to learn how to interact with peers. Led by experienced clinicians, these structured sessions focus on building everyday social abilities like taking turns, sharing, starting conversations, and understanding body language. Through fun activities and guided play, children practice new skills in real-life scenarios while forming meaningful connections with others. These groups help boost confidence and prepare children for success in school and social settings. Read More Parent Training & Support We believe parents play a important role in a child’s progress. Our Parent Training & Support program offers one-on-one guidance to help families understand and apply effective strategies at home. You’ll learn practical tools for managing behavior, encouraging communication, and building routines that support your child’s growth. Our team is here to empower you with knowledge, confidence, and ongoing support every step of the way. Behavior Assessments (FBA/BIP) Understanding the “why” behind behavior is key to meaningful progress. Our Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) conduct Functional Behavior Assessments (FBA) to identify the root causes of challenging behaviors. Based on the findings, we develop customized Behavior Intervention Plans (BIP) that focus on teaching positive alternatives and promoting long-term success at home, school, and in the community. Communication & Daily Living Skills We help children develop the skills they need to thrive in everyday life. Our therapists work on improving expressive and receptive communication so children can clearly share their needs and understand others. Sessions also focus on daily living tasks like getting dressed, brushing teeth, eating meals, and following routines, building confidence and independence step by step. School Consultation Services We partner with schools to help students with autism succeed in the classroom. Our experienced team offers support through behavior consultations, teacher and staff training, and personalized intervention strategies that fit the unique needs of each student and learning environment.
