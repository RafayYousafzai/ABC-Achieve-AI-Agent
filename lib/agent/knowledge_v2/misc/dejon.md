Skip to content Dejon Dejon has worked with children, teens, and young adults with Autism over his career in their homes, as part of early intervention cases, vocational cases and as a Teacher Assistant in an Autism Academy. Dejon was a part of a program for families with autism through the Queens College SIBS program, where he assisted children with ASD and their siblings to socialize and increase skill sets. He is pursuing a Graduate degree in ABA to become a BCBA.
