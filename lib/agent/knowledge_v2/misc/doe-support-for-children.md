Our expansion into DOE-related services allows families to access expert ABA therapy during one of the most critical times in their educational journey. What Is DOE Support for Children? When children with autism or developmental delays face legal disputes regarding their education through the DOE, it can delay the services they need. DOE support refers to specialized programs and interventions such as ABA therapy that are made available through agreements with the Department of Education, often guided by litigation or settlement. Achievement Behavior Services works alongside attorneys and families to provide these services with compassion, precision, and experience. Our goal is to ensure no child is left behind due to system delays or educational gaps.
