Skip to content Cindy Cindy oversees recruiting, interviewing, and evaluating potential new staff. She also provides case management of clients participating in the program. Cindy acts as a liaison for our families, and therapists. Cindy is very efficient, dedicated, empathetic, and always has a listening ear. She’s great at matching our families with the right therapist that will click well with the client and bring positive ABA therapy results. She helps families choose their therapy schedules and directs their clinical concerns to the appropriate parties. She’s also there for our therapists and directs any of their questions or concerns to the right team members.
