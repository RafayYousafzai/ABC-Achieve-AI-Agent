Skip to content Congratulations to Virginia Tronlone: A New RBT with a Wealth of Experience ADAM March 12, 2025 3 mins read Table of Contents We are thrilled to announce that Virginia Tronlone, a dedicated educator with over 18 years of experience in public schools, has recently passed her Registered Behavior Technician (RBT) exam! This achievement is especially remarkable given Virginia’s extensive background, holding a Master’s degree in Special Education and her Teacher of the Handicapped (TOH) certification. While she has been shaping young minds for nearly two decades, obtaining her RBT certification marks a new and exciting chapter in her career. A Career Dedicated to Helping Others Virginia has already made a significant impact in the field of education, and her decision to pursue the RBT certification demonstrates her commitment to continuing education and enhancing her ability to support students with special needs. We are confident that her deep knowledge and experience will serve her well in this new role, enabling her to make an even more significant difference in the lives of those she works with. A Celebration of Achievement We sent Virginia some special swag to celebrate her accomplishment as a token of our appreciation and to show our support for her professional development. We are delighted to share a picture of her proudly wearing the shirt we sent! The Power of Lifelong Learning Virginia’s journey is a testament to the value of lifelong learning and the importance of growing, even after many years in the field. Her story inspires educators everywhere who strive to expand their skills and knowledge for the benefit of their students. Once again, congratulations to Virginia Tronlone on her RBT certification! We are honored to have such a dedicated and passionate individual in our community, and we eagerly anticipate all the wonderful things she will accomplish in this new role. A Call to Action: Consider Your Own RBT Journey Virginia’s accomplishment is a powerful reminder that pursuing new certifications and expanding your skill set is never too late. Whether you’re just starting your career or have years of experience like Virginia, becoming an RBT can open doors to new opportunities and allow you to make an even more significant impact in the lives of those you serve. The RBT certification enhances your professional credentials and equips you with the tools and knowledge to better support individuals with special needs. We encourage anyone passionate about making a difference in behavioral health or education to consider pursuing their RBT certification. Your journey could be the next success story that inspires others!
